# Content Calendar — BJJ Open Mat (first 12 weeks)

> Cadence: **~4 scheduled posts/week** across IG + TikTok + YT Shorts, plus **daily Stories**
> and **3–5 manual community touches/week** (Reddit/FB — never scheduled).
> Post IDs (e.g. `U1`, `C3`) map to pieces in [`content-bank.md`](./content-bank.md).

## Weekly rhythm (repeatable template)

| Day | Slot | Pillar | Format | Primary platform |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | AM | Utility | Reel / Short | IG + TikTok + YT |
| **Tue** | AM | Culture / relatable | Reel or carousel | IG + TikTok |
| **Wed** | AM | Social proof | Feed post / carousel | IG + FB Page |
| **Thu** | — | *(Manual)* community day | Reddit "train while visiting" + FB group help | Reddit / FB |
| **Fri** | AM | Product / CTA | Reel or carousel | IG + TikTok + YT |
| **Daily** | — | Stories: polls, BTS, reposts, countdowns | Story | IG + FB |

Weighting over 12 weeks: **Utility 30% · Culture 30% · Social proof 20% · Product 20%.**
Gym-owner ("claim your mat") content gets **1 dedicated post/week** (rotates through the Fri slot).

## Scheduling notes
- Batch-film 2 weeks of clips in one session; schedule via Buffer/Metricool (see `tooling.md`).
- Post times (US-wide, ET-anchored): **11:30a–1p** and **6–8p** test windows; let analytics refine.
- Reels/TikTok/Shorts are the **same vertical video** repurposed 3×; adjust caption per platform.
- **Never** schedule Reddit/FB-group posts. Those are live, human, and rules-checked each time.
- Keep a 20% "reactive" buffer for trends, sounds, and community moments.
