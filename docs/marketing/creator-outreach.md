# Micro-Creator Outreach — BJJ Open Mat

> For seeding launch-day creator posts (see [`marketing-plan.md`](./marketing-plan.md) §6, the 10%
> creator line). Target **micro-creators** (5K–100K) in BJJ — they have higher engagement, lower
> cost, and more trust than big accounts. Prioritize creators whose audience actually *trains*.

---

## Who to target

| Tier | Followers | Typical deal | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Nano** | 1K–10K | Free access + gift card / gear swap | Authentic reviews, volume |
| **Micro** | 10K–100K | Flat fee (small) or affiliate/perks | Launch-day reach + trust |
| **Niche authority** | any size, high trust | Product collab / spotlight | Gym owners, coaches, travelers |

**Where to find them:** search hashtags from [`content-bank.md`](./content-bank.md) (`#bjjtravel`,
`#bjjlife`, `#openmat`), TikTok "Brazilian Jiu Jitsu" creators, YouTube Shorts grapplers, and
travel-focused grapplers (BJJ Globetrotter types) — they embody the core use case.

**Vet before you reach out:** real engagement (comments, not just likes), audience that trains,
no fake-follower signals, brand-safe content.

---

## Deal structures (pick per creator)
- **Gifting / access:** free founding access + a small gift card or gear. Cheapest; works for nanos.
- **Flat sponsored post:** one Reel/TikTok on launch day. Agree deliverable + date + usage rights in writing.
- **Affiliate / referral:** unique link or code; pay per install/signup. Aligns incentives; needs tracking.
- **Founding Creator collab:** ongoing spotlight + a co-branded "creator's open mat" feature in-app.

> Always disclose: creators must tag `#ad` / `#sponsored`. Get permission + usage rights to repost their content.

## The creator brief (send once they say yes)

**Product in one line:** Find a BJJ open mat anywhere, see who's going, tap "I'm going."

**Must-hit points (their words, not scripted):**
- It finds open mats near you *or in any city you travel to*
- You can see *who's going* (with belt rank) and RSVP
- It's free + community-driven

**Suggested angle (let them make it theirs):** their own "finding a mat while traveling" story, or a
screen-demo of finding a roll in a new city.

**Deliverable:** 1 Reel/TikTok (15–30s), posted [DATE, launch morning if paid]. Tag `@bjjopenmat` +
`#ad`. Include the link/code: [unique link].

**Do / don't:** Do be honest, show the app, keep it native to your style. Don't over-script or make it
feel like a commercial — your audience trusts you *because* you're real.

**Assets provided:** logo, screenshots, 20-sec demo clip, talking points. Reuse anything from the kit.

## Tracking
- Give each creator a **unique referral link (UTM) or code** so you can attribute installs/signups.
- Log in a simple sheet: creator · handle · followers · deal · cost · post date · link · signups · CPI.
- Post-launch: compute cost-per-signup per creator; the winners become your ongoing Founding Creators.

## Guardrails
- Disclose all paid partnerships (`#ad`). Never buy fake engagement or pressure edits that make it dishonest.
- Get written usage rights before you repost a creator's content to your own channels.
- Quality over quantity: 5–10 genuinely-aligned micro-creators beat 50 random big accounts.
