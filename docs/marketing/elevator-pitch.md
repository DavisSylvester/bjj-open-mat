# Elevator Pitch — BJJ Open Mat

## One-liner (bio / tagline)
**Find a BJJ open mat anywhere, see who's going, and tap "I'm going."**

## Short (10 sec)
BJJ Open Mat is the fastest way to find a place to roll — near you or in any city you're
traveling to. Filter by gi/no-gi, distance, and time; see who's coming; RSVP and check in.

## 30-second (the standard pitch)
Every grappler knows the problem: you're traveling, or your gym's closed, and finding an open
mat means DMing five gyms and hoping someone answers. **BJJ Open Mat** fixes that. Open the app
and it instantly maps open mats near you — or in any city — filtered by gi/no-gi, distance, skill
level, and when you want to train. You see *who's going*, tap "I'm going," and check in when you
arrive. It's community-driven: anyone can add a mat, and gyms verify their own sessions. Think of
it as the social map for open mat — built by a grappler, for grapplers. It's free.

## Gym-owner variant (30 sec)
Your open mat is one of the best tools you have for community and new members — but only if people
know it exists. **BJJ Open Mat** puts your open mat on the map for free. Travelers and locals find
you, RSVP, and show up; you verify your own sessions and see expected attendance before the doors
open. No cost, more bodies on the mat, and you stay in control of your listing. Claim your gym and
be one of our Founding Gyms.

## Investor / "why now" variant (30 sec)
BJJ is one of the fastest-growing participation sports, and its culture runs on open mats — free,
social, travel-friendly training. Yet there's no dedicated way to find them; people rely on
scattered Facebook posts and word of mouth. **BJJ Open Mat** is the map + social layer for open
mat: discovery by location, RSVPs, check-ins, and gym-owned listings. We win density gym by gym,
the RSVP/check-in social graph creates retention and network effects, and gym owners give us
supply for free because it fills their mats. It's a wedge into the broader "where and with whom do
I train" problem for a passionate, high-retention community.

## Message pillars (keep repeating)
1. **Travel & train anywhere** — the killer use case.
2. **See who's going** — the social layer nobody else has.
3. **Open mat is sacred** — built by grapplers, for grapplers.
4. **Gyms list free** — supply-side, zero cost, more bodies on the mat.
