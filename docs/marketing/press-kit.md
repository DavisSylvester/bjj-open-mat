## The one-liner
**BJJ Open Mat is the social map for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu open mats — find one anywhere, see who's going, tap "I'm going."**

## Boilerplate (drop into any article)
> BJJ Open Mat is a free, community-driven mobile app that helps grapplers find open mats near them
> or in any city they're traveling to. Users filter by gi/no-gi, distance, skill level, and time;
> see who's attending each session (with belt rank); RSVP with "I'm going"; and check in on arrival.
> Gym owners list and verify their open mats for free. Built by a grappler, for grapplers. Available
> on iOS and Android.

## Why it matters (the story angle)
BJJ is one of the fastest-growing participation sports, and its culture runs on **open mats** —
free, social, travel-friendly training. Yet there's no dedicated way to find them: people rely on
scattered Facebook posts and word of mouth. BJJ Open Mat is the discovery + social layer that
belonged to the sport already. The hook journalists like: *"the app that lets you roll in any city
you land in."*

## What's genuinely new
- **The social layer:** most tools show a gym pin; BJJ Open Mat shows *who's going* to a specific session, with belt rank.
- **Community-driven supply:** anyone can add a mat; gyms verify their own sessions — so it fills out fast and stays accurate.
- **Travel-first:** built around the real pain of finding mat time on the road.
