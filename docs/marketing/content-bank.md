## Community reply templates (manual — Reddit / FB / comments)

> Use as a starting point; **always** personalize and follow each community's self-promo rules.
> When in doubt, be useful first and only mention the app if it genuinely answers the question.

- **"Where can I train in \<city\>?"** → "A few gyms run open mats there — [name 1–2 if you know]. I've been building a free app that maps open mats by city with times + who's going if it helps: \<link\>. Either way, enjoy the rounds!"
- **"Visiting \<city\>, gi or no-gi spots?"** → Give 2 genuine suggestions, then optionally: "I keep a list of open mats by city in an app I'm building (free/beta) — happy to DM the link if useful."
- **Gym owner thread** → "If you want free exposure for your open mat, you can claim a listing on the app I'm building — travelers find you and RSVP. No cost. Link if you want it."
- **Skeptic / "another app?"** → Acknowledge honestly: "Fair! It scratches my own itch — I kept missing mats while traveling. It's free and in beta; feedback welcome, ignore if not your thing."
