## How it works (3 steps)

**1. Find a mat**
Open the app and see open mats near you by GPS, city, or ZIP — up to 100 miles. Filter by
gi/no-gi, free sessions, skill level, and when you want to train.

**2. See who's going**
Every session shows who's coming, with belt rank. RSVP with "I'm going," spot your teammates,
and know what you're walking into.

**3. Roll & check in**
Check in when you arrive and log your training. Build your streak, one roll at a time.

## FAQ

**Is it free?** Yes — for practitioners and gyms.

**When does it launch?** We're in beta now. Join the list and you'll be first in line on the App
Store and Google Play, with founding-member perks.

**What is an "open mat"?** Open training time — usually low-cost or free — where you roll without a
structured class. Visitors are often welcome. It's the heart of BJJ culture.

**How do you know a gym's info is right?** Anyone can add a mat, and gym owners verify their own
sessions so details stay accurate.

**I'm a gym owner — what's the catch?** None. Listing is free; it fills your open mats.
