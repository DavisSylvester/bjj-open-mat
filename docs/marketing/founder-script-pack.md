# Founder Content Script Pack — BJJ Open Mat

> Talking-head + voiceover scripts for the founder posts. Keep it real and unpolished — grapplers
> trust a guy in a gi at his gym over a slick ad. Shoot vertical (9:16), good light, gym in frame.
> Timings assume a natural pace (~2.5 words/sec). First 1–2 seconds = the hook, always.

## Delivery notes (read once)
- **Hook first, always.** Say the most interesting line before you introduce yourself.
- Look at the lens, not the screen. One take, minor stumbles are fine (they read as authentic).
- Add **on-screen captions** (85% watch muted). Bold the hook line.
- End with **one** clear CTA. Don't stack asks.
- Batch-film these in one session in your gi at the gym.

---

## Script 1 — "Why I built this" (S1 founder story) · ~30s
**Hook (text + spoken):** "I kept missing open mats every time I traveled — so I built an app to fix it."

> "If you train, you know the feeling. You're in a new city for work, you've got a free evening,
> and you *want* to roll — but finding an open mat means DMing five gyms and hoping someone
> answers. Half the time I just gave up.
>
> So I built **BJJ Open Mat**. Open it, and it shows you every open mat near you — or in whatever
> city you're in. Filter gi or no-gi, see who's going, tap 'I'm going,' and check in when you get
> there. Built by a grappler, for grapplers.
>
> It's in beta right now. If that's a problem you've had too, link's in my bio — join the founding
> list and help me shape it. Appreciate you. Oss."

**CTA:** Join the founding list — link in bio.

## Script 2 — "The 20-second demo" (P2, founder VO) · ~25s
**Hook:** "Watch me find a place to roll in a city I've never trained in — in 20 seconds."

> "Okay — new city, don't know a single gym here. Open the app… there's every open mat near me.
> I only do no-gi today, so — filter. Free sessions — filter. This one's a mile away, tonight at
> six. Let's see who's going… cool, couple purple belts, a few white belts. Tap 'I'm going.'
> Done. That's it. Twenty seconds, and I've got a roll tonight."

**CTA:** Comment "MAT" and I'll send you the link.
**Shot note:** screen-record the phone; founder VO over it.

## Script 3 — "Gym owners, this one's for you" (P5/S2) · ~30s
**Hook:** "Gym owners — your open mat should be on the map, and it's free."

> "Quick one for the coaches and gym owners. Your open mat is one of the best tools you've got for
> community and for pulling in new members — but only if people know it's happening.
>
> On BJJ Open Mat you list your open mat free. Travelers passing through, locals looking for extra
> rounds — they find you, they RSVP, they show up. You verify your own sessions, and you can see
> how many people are coming before you even open the doors.
>
> No cost. More bodies on your mat. First hundred gyms to claim a listing get a Founding Gym badge
> and a spotlight. Link's in my bio — go claim yours."

**CTA:** Claim your gym — link in bio.
