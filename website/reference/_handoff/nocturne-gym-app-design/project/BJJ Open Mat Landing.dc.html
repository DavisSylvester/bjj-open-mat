How it works

01

Find a mat

Open the app and see open mats around you by GPS, city, or ZIP — up to 100 miles out. Filter by gi or no-gi, free sessions, skill level, and when you want to train.

02

See who's going

Every session shows who's coming, with belt rank. RSVP with "I'm going," spot your teammates, and know what you're walking into before you drive over.

03

Roll and check in

Check in when you arrive and log your training. Rate the mat, build your streak, and keep a record of every roll — home or on the road.

For gym owners

Put your open mat on the map. Registering is free — being found is free.

Your open mat is one of your best tools for community and new members — if people know it exists. Registering your gym costs nothing, and it's free for practitioners to find you. Travelers and locals discover your mat, RSVP, and show up. You verify your own sessions and see expected attendance before the doors open.

First 100 gyms get the Founding Gym badge and a featured spotlight, forever.
